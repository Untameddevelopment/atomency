Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 16, 2026

Atomency ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of students, teachers, and educational institutions. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information in connection with our educational chemistry platform.

gavel 1. FERPA Compliance

Atomency operates in compliance with the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (20 U.S.C. ยง 1232g; 34 CFR Part 99). We do not collect, maintain, or disclose student education records as defined by FERPA.

Our platform does not require student login, store personal information, or track individual activity. No student personally identifiable information (PII) is collected or stored.

block 2. Information We Do Not Collect

We explicitly do NOT collect:

  • Student names or identification numbers
  • Home or email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Grades, test scores, or academic records
  • Behavioral records or disciplinary information
  • IP addresses linked to specific user sessions
  • Cookies for individual identification purposes
  • Any data that could identify an individual student

analytics 3. Limited Data Collection

Atomency operates as a serverless static application. We collect only anonymous aggregate data such as:

  • Total page views (no user identifiers)
  • General feature usage statistics
  • Technical performance metrics

This data is used solely for technical maintenance and platform improvement. No individual user activity is tracked or stored.

storage 4. Local Storage

All user-generated content is stored exclusively in the user's browser localStorage. This includes:

  • Molecular structures created in the simulator
  • Exported molecule files
  • User preferences and settings

We cannot access, retrieve, or view any data stored in your browser's localStorage. This data remains on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

cloud_queue 5. Third-Party Services

Atomency integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Netlify: Web hosting and deployment. Netlify may collect standard server logs for technical purposes only.
  • PubChem PUG REST API: Used for compound lookup and chemical data enrichment. API requests do not include user identifiers.

No third-party analytics (Google Analytics, etc.) or advertising services are integrated into Atomency.

security 6. Data Security

Since Atomency does not collect or store personal user data, there is minimal security risk. However, we implement industry-standard security practices for our hosting infrastructure, including HTTPS encryption and secure deployment protocols.

child_care 7. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Atomency is designed for students aged 13 and above in alignment with K-12 educational standards. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe such data has been collected inadvertently, please contact us immediately at help@atomency.com.

verified_user 8. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal information, there is no user data to access, modify, or delete. Any data stored in your browser's localStorage is entirely under your control and can be cleared at any time through your browser settings.

cookie 9. Cookies and Tracking

Atomency does not use tracking cookies or any similar tracking technologies. Cookies used by the platform are strictly functional (e.g., maintaining session state for the application) and do not collect or transmit personal information.

public 10. International Data Transfers

Since Atomency does not collect personal user data, there are no international data transfers of personal information. The platform operates as a static application delivered via Content Delivery Network (CDN).

update 11. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, legal requirements, or for other operational reasons. We will notify users of any material changes by posting the updated policy on this page with a revised "Effective Date."
